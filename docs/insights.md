# Dashboard Insights

## Overview

This dashboard provides insights into selected technology stocks using price trends, trading volume, daily movement, intraday return, and volatility indicators. The dashboard is designed to support both individual stock analysis and multi-company comparison.

## Key Insights

### 1. Stock Price Trend Analysis

The Closing Price Trend visual helps identify whether a selected stock is moving upward, downward, or sideways during the rolling date window.

Users can compare price movement over time and understand how the selected stock has performed across recent trading days.

### 2. Trading Volume Analysis

The Trading Volume Trend visual shows how actively a stock is being traded.

Higher trading volume may indicate increased investor interest, market reaction to news, earnings activity, or broader market movement.

### 3. Open vs Close Price Movement

The Open vs Close Price Trend helps compare where a stock started and ended each trading day.

If the close price is consistently higher than the open price, it may indicate positive intraday movement. If the close price is frequently lower than the open price, it may indicate weaker daily performance.

### 4. High vs Low Price Range

The Daily High vs Low Price Range shows the price movement within each trading day.

A wider gap between high and low prices indicates larger intraday price swings and may suggest higher volatility.

### 5. Daily Price Range

Daily Price Range measures the difference between the high and low prices for a trading day.

This metric helps identify days when the selected stock experienced stronger price movement.

### 6. Intraday Return %

Intraday Return % measures the percentage change from the opening price to the closing price.

Positive intraday return means the stock closed higher than it opened. Negative intraday return means the stock closed lower than it opened.

### 7. Risk and Volatility View

The Risk and Volatility Analysis page helps users identify periods of stronger price movement and higher short-term risk.

The page uses Daily Price Range and Intraday Return % to show how much the selected stock moved during the trading period.

### 8. Company Comparison

The Company Comparison page allows users to compare AAPL, AMZN, MSFT, NVDA, and TSLA side by side.

This page helps evaluate:

* Closing price trends by company
* Average close price by company
* Total trading volume by company
* Daily price range and intraday return by company

## Dashboard Interpretation

The dashboard should be used as a market analysis and visualization tool. It helps users explore price movement, volume activity, and volatility behavior, but it is not intended to provide investment advice.

## Business Value

This dashboard demonstrates how raw stock market data can be transformed into interactive insights using Power BI. It allows users to move from high-level market summaries to detailed stock performance analysis and company-level comparison.

## Project Conclusion

This project successfully built an API-connected Power BI dashboard for stock market intelligence. The final dashboard allows users to analyze individual stock performance, review risk and volatility indicators, and compare multiple technology companies through interactive visuals and slicers.
